Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SideKick ("we," "our," or "the app") handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data We Access

SideKick accesses the following types of data solely for app functionality:

1. Screen Time Data (via Screen Time API)

  • What: Information about which apps you've selected to block
  • Why: To apply friction shields and focus schedules
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device only
  • Sharing: Never shared with anyone, including us

2. Health Data (via HealthKit)

  • What: Your sleep duration and sleep schedule
  • Why: To provide personalized focus insights based on your rest patterns
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device only
  • Sharing: Never leaves your device or shared with third parties
  • Optional: You can deny HealthKit access and still use core app features

3. App Usage Patterns

  • What: How many times you've opened blocked apps, session durations
  • Why: To adjust adaptive friction and show usage insights
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device only
  • Sharing: Never transmitted off your device

How We Use Your Data

All data is used exclusively on your device for these purposes:

  • Applying friction shields when you try to open blocked apps
  • Managing focus schedules and time-based blocking
  • Calculating adaptive friction based on your usage patterns
  • Displaying sleep-based focus insights
  • Showing your progress and statistics in the app
Important: Because all data stays on your device, if you delete the app, all your data is permanently deleted with it.

In-App Purchases

SideKick offers optional in-app purchases (subscriptions) to unlock Pro features. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Apple may collect:

  • Purchase history
  • Payment method information
  • Billing address

Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles this data.

Third-Party Services

SideKick uses Apple's native frameworks only:

  • Screen Time API (Family Controls) - For app blocking
  • HealthKit - For sleep data (optional)
  • WidgetKit - For home screen widgets
  • StoreKit - For in-app purchases

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is viewable within the app
  • Deletion: Delete the app to remove all data permanently
  • Control: Revoke HealthKit access anytime in iOS Settings
  • Portability: Data is not exportable since it stays only on your device

Children's Privacy

SideKick is designed for individuals managing their own devices and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
